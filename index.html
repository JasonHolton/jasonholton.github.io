About

For over two decades, I dedicated myself to the world of analytical chemistry, delving into the intricate realms of food ingredients and food systems in the healthcare industry. It was a journey marked by continuous research, method development, and data analysis—a path where every discovery held significance. About a year ago, I made the challenging yet pivotal decision to step away from my role in chemistry. Uncertain of my next steps, through guidance and introspection I realized that my analytical acumen could seamlessly transition into the realm of data analytics and science. It wasn't just a career shift; it was a conscious choice—a fusion of my skill set with the flexibility to work from home, nurturing my family while continuing to unravel the captivating world of data.

With a rich background in analytical chemistry and extensive research experience in the healthcare and consumer products sector, I bring a unique perspective to the realm of data analytics and science. My journey from a subject matter expert in molecular spectroscopy, protein chemistry, and oil oxidation to a Google-certified data analyst has equipped me with a diverse skill set and an astute analytical mindset. I've honed my ability to handle vast datasets, employing resourceful and creative problem-solving methodologies to derive valuable insights. My expertise spans a spectrum of data tools including Excel, SQL, Python, R and Tableau, facilitating comprehensive data analysis and visualization.

Throughout my tenure at Abbott Nutrition, I leveraged innovative method development and rigorous data collection and analysis techniques to assess, for example, product stability and manufacturing processes. My data-driven studies spearheaded substantial cost savings, such as reevaluating refrigeration necessity during overseas shipping, saving over $500K annually. Additionally, I uncovered inaccuracies in vendor claims resulting in cost savings exceeding $2M yearly. My knack for meticulous data analysis led to the development of innovative approaches, like quantifying allergen levels in infant formula manufacturing equipment, effectively addressing technical complexities and fostering reliable solutions.

My proficiency extends beyond technical analysis—I possess a compelling knack for impactful storytelling and effective data visualization. I've authored published research projects and white papers, effectively communicating intricate technical findings to diverse audiences. Armed with certifications in data analytics, visualization, SQL, and data extraction, transformation and loading, I'm poised to deliver comprehensive and impactful insights, bridging the gap between intricate technicalities and pragmatic solutions. Explore my portfolio to witness the fusion of my analytical prowess with a profound understanding of data analytics.

Find out more at my LinkedIn and GitHub pages.
